What is HackFairview?
HackFairview is the only 24 hour high school hackathon in the Rocky Mountain region. It is hosted at Fairview High School in Boulder, Colorado. Over 200 students will come together to build whatever they want, hardware or software. Students will use collaborative effort and their thoughtful creativity to innovate, learn, and have fun.
